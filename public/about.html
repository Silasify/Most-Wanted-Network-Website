About us

A small network for players who like things handled properly

Most Wanted Network is our place for hosting games, trying new ideas, and keeping the community close enough that people recognize each other.

How we run things

Keep it fair, keep it readable, and listen when players speak up

We care about servers that feel worth joining: clear rules, active support, and no guessing game about what is going on.

We choose what to host based on what people want to play, what we can look after properly, and what has enough life in it to last.

Reliability

Live checks help players see what is online before they gather everyone for a session.

Community

Events, suggestions, tickets, and Discord tools keep the group connected between game nights.

Fair Play

Rules should protect the fun, not smother it. That is the balance we aim for.
