Suggestions

Got an idea? Send it in.

New server, event night, settings change, Discord improvement, odd little quality-of-life thing: if it would make MWN better, we want to see it.

What helps us act on it

  • What game, mode, setting, or event you have in mind.
  • Why you think people would show up for it.
  • Any useful details like mods, timing, server rules, or examples.
  • Whether you would help test, moderate, or organize it.
